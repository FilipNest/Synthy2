SYNTHY 2

Developed as a sequel to Synthy.io this is more a basic experiment in sequencing.

The squares can be linked and reordered to play chords. Durations can also be changed. Drag inside to change pitch. Originally horizontal drag was amplitude/filter cut off but now it doesn't do much.

Radio buttons are octaves.

Synthesiser sound powered by tone.js as I felt doing it myself again was unnecessary.

This may evolve. Tempted to turn it into a sampler.
